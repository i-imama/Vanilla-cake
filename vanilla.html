Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Essence
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • Milk as required
  • 1 cup Butter
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter and flour a 6-cup loaf pan; set aside.
  2. Using an electric mixer on high speed, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition; add vanilla. With mixer on low, gradually add flour, beating just until combined
  3. Add milk to balance the consistancy if the mixture is too thick.
  4. Bake until a toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean, about 1 hour. Let cool in pan for 15 minutes. Invert onto a wire rack, and turn upright to cool completely.
